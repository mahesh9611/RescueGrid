How it works

Emergency response built for speed and transparency.

  1. Patient alerts: User submits emergency type and current location.
  2. Hospital prep: Nearby hospitals receive the alert with resource needs and ETA.
  3. Ambulance route: Live GPS tracking shows ambulance progress to both patient and hospital.
  4. Ready care: Medical teams prepare ICU, blood, oxygen, and specialists before arrival.
